How it works (4 quick steps)

1) Download your data

From Instagram, export your account data as JSON (followers & following). You’ll get files like followers_1.json and following.json.

2) Drag & drop

Open the app and drag both files into the drop area (or click to select). Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

3) Compare

Click Compare Now to see Not Following You Back and You Don’t Follow Back. Use the whitelist to exclude brands/creators.

4) Export

Save clean text files of both lists for your records.

Privacy & FAQ

Do my files leave my device?

No. UnfollowGuard runs fully in your browser. It never sends your data to a server.

What file types are supported?

JSON exports from Instagram (followers, following). The app auto-detects both formats that Instagram uses.

What’s the whitelist?

A local list of usernames you want to ignore (e.g., brands/celebrities). It’s stored in localStorage so it persists on your device.
